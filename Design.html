The Body Barn

Welcome to The Body Barn, owned and operated by Sara Salingue Wilson, in Christopher, IL.

Where beauty is enhanced, stress is relieved, spirits are brightened, and closets are filled.


In early 2020, with the pandemic causing shutdowns all over the world, I was given no choice but to close the Salon and Spa due to mandated lockdowns. With a family, and not being able to maintain my income from the Salon and Spa, I faced what everyone in the world faced...How do I survive without my income. I began my search for another way to bring a smile to someone's face and started my design business with T-shirts and Blankets for the entire family - including the fur-babies.

I partnered with several friends and the Design side of The Body Barn was born. We provide T-shirts for a multitude of tastes and Blankets for all of the quarantine couch cuddling we all enjoy.

Below you will find the color swatches for any item you would wish to purchase. In the menu above, you can click on Tshirts or Blankets to see some of our common offerings. Once you have made your choice, please click on contact in the menu and email your order or send me questions and comments.

Also, please remember, you can always call me with questions and orders if you choose to do so and look us up on Facebook at The Body Barn or join our VIP Page on Facebook at The Body Barn VIP.
